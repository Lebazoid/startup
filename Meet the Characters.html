The brave young prince of Ostend! He takes pride in the title, and wants to live up to the good name. Since the age of 5 he trained in Ostend's own Knight Academy, and was consistently top of his class. His efforts were acknowledged by his father, who began training him on his own with another student, Lloyd, at the age of 12. He wields a magical flame blade that casts brilliants flames at enemies, but he seems to lag behind his classmate - a fact that frustrates him deeply. He dreams of surpassing Lloyd and proving himself to his father.

Amber is a young girl who lives peacefully in the countryside, but her heart longs for adventure. Since she was young, she had masterful control of a paintbrush, and her paintings adorn a wall in every palace in Aldea, although she goes by the pseudonym "Vinivier." When she finds Ezlo bruised and battered, she wonders if he will be the knight in shining armor that takes her on her long awaited adventure.

A mysterious and enigmatic woman with brilliant green hair, and the head of Mila's widely respected mercenary guild. She was raised by monks from a young age, and is adept at many magical arts, including wielding the divine bow Celestia. She seems to have unique connections to the Aldean Chancellor, which only makes her all the more mysterious. Can she be trusted?

Zach is a powerful wizard from Hetalia, although he can't remember much from his life before recent. He seems to be unable to control his magic, causing it to be reckless and destructive.
